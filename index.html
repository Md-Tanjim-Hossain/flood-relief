    Our Donate Plan

    Our volunteers from all over Bangladesh are working to distribute emergency relief to the victims, our Foundation volunteers are participating in the distribution of relief to the victims in a strategic plan and in an orderly manner in case of emergency. Your sympathy and kindness give us the courage to serve these flood victims. We pay our respects to all the donors who are helping us in this time of crisis.

    Food for the family

    We are distributing food on a package basis, where each relief package for flood victims includes - 5 kg of rice, 1 kg of pulses, 1 kg of beaten rice, 1 kg of onions, 3 kg of potatoes, 1 kg of salt, and water purification Tablets.

    Shelter for the family

    Our volunteers are building temporary shelters for people in flood-prone areas on high ground or on embankments, helping flood-stricken people who have no place to live at all.

    Medical for the family

    Our hardworking volunteer medical staff has set up several medical camps to treat people in flood-prone areas and distribute free medicine and provide advice on related diseases.
